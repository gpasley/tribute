Tech Billionaire

David Steward

Timeline

  • 1951 - Born in Chicago, IL
  • 1973 - Graduated from Central Missouri State University with a B.S. in Business
  • 1974 - Began work at Wagner Electric as a production manager
  • 1975 - Began work as a sales representative at Missouri Pacific Railroad Company
  • 1979 - Began work as a senior account executive at Federal Express
  • 1981 - Inducted into the Federal Express Hall of Fame
  • 1990 - Founded World Wide Technology
  • 1999 - Spun off WWT telecommunications division to form Telcobuy.com
  • 2003 - Combined revenues of companies reached $1 Billion
